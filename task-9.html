ABOUT US

Health Click DIAGNOSTICS most advanced Totally Automated Laboratory having its strong presence in india Health Click is fully automated diagnostic laboratory with a focus on providing quality at affordable costs to laboratories and hospitats in india Health Click operates in Nagpur India for esoteric tests and Regional Processing Laboratory in major metro cities of india. We have focus on strong technologies, strong brands and strong systems that enable all laboratories to give their clients the best of science and technology at affordable cost. With a belief that Quality' is the heart of any intelligent management. Our clear focus on speed and accuracy further made us the first indian laboratory to also have an IT enabled. 24x7, fully automated diagnostic laboratory set up covering over 10.000 sq. ft. floor space that ensures error free processing of over 10.000 specimens and over Clinical Chemistry investigations per month. Our unmatched speed factor is achieved through a combination of air-cargo logistics and IT enabled, barcoded, bi-directional systems that ensures a turnaround time of 4 to 8 hours for processing of samples that arrive at any time of the day or night.
Meticulously created and monitored systems, well-chosen and trained manpower, wise selection of the best global technologies for specialized testing and an uncompromised approach to instrumentation nas ensured that Health Click is looked upon as a yardstick for quality by stakeholders in the diagnostic industry. The uniqueness of Health Click is its effort to innovate and remain at par of the global standards for best of quality service delivery at the most cost-effective proces Many laboratories and hospital brands in India, Middle East and South East Asian countries, use Health Click to complete their menu and deliver quality at an affordable cost.
